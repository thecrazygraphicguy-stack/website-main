Refund Policy

Last updated: February 5, 2026

I'm a one-person shop and I sell digital products that deliver instantly. You can also hire me (if a slot is available) for custom services. Here's my policy — clear and simple.

Digital products (instant downloads)

Because digital products are delivered immediately and can't be "returned," all digital sales are final and non-refundable.

If something is broken (corrupted files, missing files, download issues), email hi@thecrazygraphicguy.com and I'll happily fix it.

Custom services (made-to-order work)

Custom work is paid 100% upfront.

  • 100% refund if you cancel before I confirm I've started working on your request

Starting work: I consider the work started only after I confirm in writing (in your Trello board status) that I've begun.

If a project is delayed because I'm waiting on details, files, or approvals from you, the timeline may shift.
